Hideaway Vacation Homes is a partnership of a group of people dedicated to providing a high level of service to our clients. We share a common vision of providing a memorable and relaxing experience, and we bring different skills and interests to our work for you.

Thomas Beebe

Thomas has a B.S. in Landscape Architecture and has always been passionate about fixing and improving his surroundings. After 6 years of post-graduate traveling in search of the perfect place to live, he moved to Hawaii in 1998.

In 2000 he began managing capital improvements to the beautiful 3 acre Kauai property that have made Plumeria Moon and Hideaway Bay so popular with guests ever since Kauai Vacation Hideaway was created.

Thomas lives on the North Shore of Kauai with his wife Lyle, son Bodhi and daughter Talia.  He manages the sales and marketing division of Hideaway Vacation Homes.

Mike Testa

Mike Testa is currently the Director of Strategic Accounts at Salesforce.com.  Mike and his team are focused on selling Customer Relationship Management (CRM) applications to global companies like HP, FedEx, Yahoo, Intel, eBay, Amazon, and National Semiconductor.  Mike also serves as the President and CEO of Hideaway Vacation Homes, www.HVH.com, providing exclusive vacation rentals around the world. 

Finally, Mike serves on the Board of the Arthur B. Schultz Foundation.  As a Trustee, Mike is involved in coordinating funding for Disabled Recreation, Global Understanding, Micro-Enterprise Lending, and Environmental Conservation initiatives.  Over the past 7 years, the ABS Foundation has given over $3.5M dollars to worthy organizations around the world.
